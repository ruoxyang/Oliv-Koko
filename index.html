Handmade Artisan Soap

Artisan Soap and Skincare Products

Oliv & Koko soaps and skincare products are formulated from the highest quality ingredients using extra virgin olive oil combined with other plant-based natural oils and butters.

The artisan soaps are all handmade using the cold process method to ensure you have a mild soap and providing a rich lather that will leave your skin gently cleansed and moisturised.

We are 100% Australian owned. Handmade in small batches to ensure freshness and quality, with no palm oil, and no animal testing.
